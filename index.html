Welcome Duelists

Are you tired of manually searching for Yu-Gi-Oh card names every time on the Neuron app or TCGPlayer? With this application, you can easily query cards with a single click and quickly get the effects of a card (when it works)!

As this is an early development idea, please let me know if you encounter any bugs or have suggestions for improvements to enhance the query functionality.

End Comments

I would also like to give a big thanks to YGO Pro for providing an extensive database of the most recent card information! Due to the requirements of their API, I have decided to download the entire database, and it will need to be manually updated.

To start, click on "Search Card" and say "card" followed by the entire card name (e.g., "card Blue-Eyes White Dragon"). If the query is correct, an enlarged photo of the card will appear, and a voice assistant will explain the card's effect. I hope you all enjoy using this application I just thought of randomly!
